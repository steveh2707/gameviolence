Evaluation

Your performance will be assessed according to the following criteria:

Components Assessment details Level of achievement
Nonverbal skills (30%) Eye contacts: look at individuals in turns or look at the whole 1 - 10
Body language: movements of the body 1 - 10
Posture: confident, calm 1 - 10
Verbal skills (30%) Language: formal/informal, signposting, language functions 1 - 15
Harmony: what you say and what you show must go together 1 - 15
Contents (40%) Organisation: easy to follow, confusing 1 - 10
Mechanics: with/without visual aids/graphs/charts 1 - 10
